AGAINST UKRAINIAN WOMAN

TOGETHER

During Russian invasion, hundreds of Ukrainian women are being raped, tortured, and killed.

On May 2022, the European Parliament’s resolution “The impact of the war against Ukraine on women” confirmed the violation of women’s rights caused by Russian army.

What We Stand For

Prevention

Convince your authorities to review and improve the mechanisms preventing nations from such atrocities in future wars.

Reaction

Spread the truth about Russian atrocities to convince the EU to help refugee women and arm Ukraine so that it could stop the Russian aggression.

Support

Donate to the organizations helping women who suffered from Russian aggression overcome their traumas and move on in life.
